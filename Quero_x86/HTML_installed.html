Hints and Tips

Tip 1: Replace the Address Bar with Quero

IE11, IE10, IE9: uncheck the option Quero > Options > Appearance > IE address box / navigations buttons
IE8, IE7: uncheck the option Quero > Options > Appearance > IE navigation and tab bar
IE6: uncheck View > Toolbars > Address Bar

Tip 2: Move or Resize the Toolbar

Quero can share its space with other 3rd-party toolbars and the Menu bar or reduce the entire interface to one line.

Tip 3: Configure Quero

Click on the Quero button (Alt+Q) to customize your toolbar and to access the toolbar functions.
